Our Coffee

Counter Culture


It is of utmost concern that the coffee and milk experience we provide is not only quality, but protects the environment, while paying fare wages to the farmer who labor it. Counter Culture Coffee is most known for it's 19 years of experience in roasting specialty grade coffee. However, their reputation is equally celebrated due to their coffee sourcing. It is through their efforts that Direct Relationships and Sustainable Agriculture are jargon linked to the western coffee industy setting new expectations for how coffee is purchased from foreign countries has renovated communities and enriched the lives of many coffee farmers globally.
